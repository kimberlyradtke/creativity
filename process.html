Creative Process

When I am presented with a design problem, I first make sure that I understand what exactly is being asked of me. What am I trying to accomplish? Next I start to sketch out all of my ideas. I work to refine those sketches until I get something that I like. After I get some sketches, I start to put it onto the computer. The sketched ideas are refined at this point and I work and work until I come up with a final product.

Future Improvements

In the past, I never sketched out my designs. I would always visual something and then go straight to the program to start my creation. Now, I have gotten into the habit of sketching out some ideas first. I can't believe how much it helps me. It gives me a visual ahead of time. I feel like I am saving a lot of time now, because I can see my design concept ahead of time instead of designing and finding out that it does not work in the end. I would like to continue to sketch more in the future. Another step that I would like to add into my creative process is to create wire-frames. I think this would help to visual space better and how everything is going to fit together without having to worry about the aesthetics.

Creative Goals

My creative goals include broadening my artistic background through classes and practice, training myself to think outside of the box, and becoming more technical in web design.
